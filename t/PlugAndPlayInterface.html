Plug and Play Hardware Interface

Could it be any simpler than plugging your hardware into evive and controlling directly from the on-screen user interface. evive provide direct outputs for controlling DC motor, DC motor driver, servo motor, stepper motor, relay and pnuematics.

DC Motor

evive has two inbuilt motor control units with which you can control motors with current limit of 1A each. It uses a SN754410NE Quad H-Bridge IC to control two DC Motors.

Relay
