Introduction

evive has inbuilt 5-way navigational, which is used to navigate in evive menu based system. The navigational key have 4 directions: left, right, up and down. This information is provided by the analog pin A11.

The center switch is connected to the digital pin 19 in active high mode. This means that if the switch is pressed, then pin 19 is high, otherwise the pin is LOW.

The table provide you the corresponding values for directions:

Direction

Analog value range

Up

205-215

Right

405-415

Down

605-615

Left

835-845
