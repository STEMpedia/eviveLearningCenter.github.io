Robotic Arm

evive's learning modules are designed to give you a perfect learning experience. Use these easy to assemble DIY modules to make your perfect robot mate, helpingy ou do things otherwise difficult to accomplish.

The Robotic Arm module enables a multitude of possibilities- make your personal pick-and-place system or a sketching robot to draw your favourite cartoon character or make a candy sorter. Try to assemble the module yourself or take help of the instructions inside. Use evive's menu based interface to control your creation or program it to automate the task.

Tutorials

This learning module allows you to make two different kind of robots, a picking and placing robotic arm and a sketching robotic arm. You can also add another end effector and use it as a generic SCARA robot.

ABOUT THE PROJECT
This is a four degree of freedom robotic arm capable of picking and placing small objects (up to 50g). With 15 cm long links, the robotic arm can fit perfectly on your table top, use the clip to attach it to your table or use screws if you’re comfortable with drilling holes.
PROJECT INFO
Programming Platform: Arduino Type: Tutorial Difficulty: Intermediate Estimated Time: 10 hours

ABOUT THE PROJECT
This is a three degree of freedom robotic arm with a sketch pen holder at it’s end. You can code this robot to draw anything, write your name, sketch your face and much more.
PROJECT INFO
Programming Platform: Arduino Type: Tutorial Difficulty: Intermediate Estimated Time: 10 hours
