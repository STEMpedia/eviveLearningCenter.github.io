Assembly Instructions:

  1. Gently insert a metal servo motor (the big one) inside the Base Mount. First insert the motor from the wire side and push it (the wire will otherwise cause probles in fitting).
    Once the servo motor is inserted, mount motor to the base plate using 12mm M4 bolts.
Note: In case you find it difficult mounting the servo this way, try inserting it from below while ensuring that servo’s top face is parallel to mount’s top face after fastening them together. Errors in this mounting will heavily impact clearance of end effector from your table top.
  1. Similarly insert and mount the other metal servo motor inside Link 1 using same 12mm M4 bolts.
  2. Metal servo comes along with mounting accessories, take two screws and a servo horn from it and attach it to Link 1 as shown in the figure below.
  3. Take three 10mm M3 Standoffs and mount them on Link 1 on the holes shown below, use M3 Nuts for fastening.

This is how the First link should look after step 4.

  1. Connect the Base Servo (from Step 1) to evive (on S1/S2 port) and use evive’s menu to set the servo angle at 90 degrees.
Failing to do this will rotate the arm in undesirable directions and may damage the arm and things around it. Keep in mind that ground (darkest wire of the three, most commonly brown) of the servo must be connected to the left-most of the three headers in evive’s port.

Now mount Link 1 to the Base Servo as shown. Use a black screw from servo accessories to fasten Link 1 to the Base Servo.

Keep in mind, if the screw is not tight enough, Link 1 might get bent from hinge during operation.
  1. In order to strengthen Link 1, Support Link 1 should be mounted on Standoffs shown using 8mm M3 bolts.
  2. Mount Micro Servo Holder on Link 2 using 8mm M3 bolts and M3 nuts.
  3. Insert Micro Servo in the holder and mount it using 8mm M2 bolts and M2 nuts. Mount the servo horn on the other end of link 2 (similar to Step 3).
  4. Mount 10mm M3 standoffs on remaining holes using M3 nuts (similar to Step 4).
  5. Insert Link 2 on Metal Servo of the Link 1 and tighten it using the screw provided with Metal Servo (similar to Step 5).
  6. Mount the Support Link 2 on top of the standoffs using 8mm M3 bolts (similar to Step 6).
  7. Take Gripper Claws, Gripper Link and Micro Servo Horn, and assemble them using 12mm M3 bolts for Gripper Claws and Gripper Link, M2 servo screw for Gripper Link and Servo Mount.
  8. Mount micro servo horn to Pen Holder using screws provided with motor.
  9. Attach the Pen Holder to the micro servo of Link 2 using the servo horn.
  10. Insert the Pen inside the Pen Holder and tighten it with an 8mm M3 bolt.
