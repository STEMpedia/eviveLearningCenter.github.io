DC Motor Driver Pin Output

evive has a dedicated pair of DC Motor Driver pin outputs, which can be used when user have to connect a large motor which draw current more than 1 Amp.

The pin configuration from left to right is:

  1. GND
  2. VVR
  3. PWM Pin (For channel 1, connected to digital pin 44 to evive, and for channel 2, connected to digital pin 45)
  4. Direction Pin 1 (For channel 1, connected to digital pin 28 to evive, and for channel 2, connected to digital pin 30)
  5. Direction Pin 1 (For channel 1, connected to digital pin 29 to evive, and for channel 2, connected to digital pin 31)
